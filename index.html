How Results Are Calculated

The scoring system behind your personality type

The Three Axes (SFW)

The quiz measures your personality across three independent axes. Each axis has 4 questions with answers scored from +2 to -2. Your total score on each axis determines which side you fall on.

Social Style

Pack (P)
Solitary (S)

Score ≥ 0 = Pack • Score < 0 = Solitary

Energy Level

Playful (Y)
Calm (C)

Score ≥ 0 = Playful • Score < 0 = Calm

Environment

Wild (W)
Cozy (Z)

Score ≥ 0 = Wild • Score < 0 = Cozy

Intimacy Adjective (18+ Mode)

In 18+ mode, 8 additional questions measure two extra dimensions: an intimacy score (dominant vs submissive) and a kink score. These combine to produce one of 6 adjectives that prefix your personality type.

Dominant

Intimacy ≥ 2, kink between -3 and 3

Submissive

Intimacy ≤ -2, kink between -3 and 3

Switch

Intimacy between -1 and 1, kink between -3 and 3

Kinky

Kink ≥ 3 (at any intimacy level)

Vanilla

Kink ≤ -3 (when intimacy is between -5 and 1)

Playful

Defined but not currently assigned by the scoring logic

The kink score acts as an override: high kink always yields Kinky regardless of dom/sub position, while very low kink yields Vanilla for mid-range intimacy scores.
